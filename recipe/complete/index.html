Mom's Pancakes

These pancakes have been a treat in my family for generations. It reminds me of home and the hot texan sun. It pains my mother to think anyone would use a batter mix when these are so simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Yield: 4 Servings
    •
  • 2 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Baking Powcer
  • A dash of Salt
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 6 Tbsp Melted Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix together all the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Form the dry mixture into a mound and then form a well in the center of the mound.
  3. Pour the milk and eggs into the well you formed. Beat the eggs with the milk and slowly mix it in with the dry ingredients.
  4. Once the batter looks fully mixed, fold in the melted butter.
  5. Cook them on a griddle or pan greased with butter, on medium heat.
  6. Finally, enjoy breakfast with your family :)
